About this project

Why did I choose this project?

I chose to do an instructable on a charcoal drawing because I recently took a drawing class on it.
Art has always been a part of my life and it is a passion of mine. The drawing shown in this project is of my
Grandpa. I gifted it to him and my Grandma on Christmas.

Where can you learn more?
