About the Author

Olena Kassian


Olena Kassian creative path has taken her from illustration for book publishing, to advertising illustration
with clients such as Nestle's and Del Monte, and exhibitions of her large figurative drawings on Mylar. "Nicholas,
you are ridiculous!" is her 17th children's book, based on a long relationship with her Shiba Inu, Nikki.
She has two adult children, and lives in Toronto.
