What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?
An IP address is the direct address for a server. The DNS is the Domain Name System and its like an address book that our browser uses to locate the IP addresses to the sites we are trying to visit.

What is a cookie?
A cookie is a packet of data that gets stored on your computer when you visit a site. Next time you visit the site it uses the cookie to keep record of you and your activity on the site.
